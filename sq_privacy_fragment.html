Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Read our Terms of Service →

At Marge Games ("Marge Games," "we," "us," or "our"), we make lighthearted games for the way you actually hang out — from cozy mobile apps like Snack Society to tabletop games for game night. We care about your privacy as much as we care about a good time. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

This policy applies to our mobile and digital applications, our tabletop games and related materials, and our website (collectively, the "Services"). By using the Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

A quick, human summary: We don't sell your personal information. We collect the minimum we need to run our games and website, answer your messages, and improve our products. You can contact us anytime to access or delete your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you give us

  • Contact & messages. If you fill out our contact form or email us, we collect your name, email address, and whatever you choose to write.
  • Mailing list. If you sign up to hear about new games (like our Bookish waitlist), we collect your email address.
  • Purchases & shop. If you buy physical games or merchandise from us, we (or our payment and fulfillment providers) collect the information needed to process and ship your order, such as your name, shipping address, and contact details. Payment card details are handled by our payment processor — we do not store full card numbers.

Information collected automatically (digital apps & website)

  • Usage & device data. When you use our apps or website, we and our service providers may collect technical information such as device type, operating system, app version, general location (e.g., country), and how you interact with the Services.
  • Analytics & performance. We may use analytics and crash-reporting tools (for example, those provided by Apple or trusted third parties) to understand how our games are used and to fix bugs.
  • In-app purchases. Purchases made inside our apps are processed by the relevant app store (such as the Apple App Store). The app store — not Marge Games — handles your payment information; we receive limited transaction details such as confirmation that a purchase occurred.
  • Cookies & similar technologies. Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to remember preferences and measure traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Tabletop games

Our physical tabletop games generally do not collect any personal information on their own. We only receive information about you in connection with tabletop games if you contact us, sign up for updates, register a product, or buy from our shop.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve our games, apps, and website;
  • To respond to your questions, feedback, and support requests;
  • To send you updates you've asked for (like new-game announcements), which you can opt out of at any time;
  • To process and deliver orders from our shop;
  • To understand how our Services are used so we can make them better;
  • To keep our Services safe, prevent abuse, and comply with our legal obligations.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited situations:

  • Service providers. With trusted companies that help us run the Services — for example, analytics providers, email and hosting services, app stores, and payment/shipping providers — and only as needed to perform their work for us.
  • Legal reasons. When we believe disclosure is required to comply with the law, enforce our terms, or protect the rights, safety, and property of Marge Games, our players, or others.
  • Business transfers. If Marge Games is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for a general audience and are not directed to children under 13 (or the age defined by your local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at liam@hedyverse.com and we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

If any of our games are ever specifically directed to children, we will provide additional protections and parental-consent mechanisms as required by applicable laws such as the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Your Choices & Rights

  • Email updates. You can unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time using the link in our emails or by contacting us.
  • Access & deletion. You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you.
  • Device controls. You can manage app permissions, advertising identifiers, and analytics options through your device or browser settings.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) or the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to know what personal information we collect and to request its deletion. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at liam@hedyverse.com.

6. Data Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as needed to provide the Services and for legitimate business or legal purposes. When we no longer need it, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize it.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-Party Services

Our Services may link to or rely on third-party services (such as the Apple App Store, analytics tools, and payment processors). Their handling of your information is governed by their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date above, and significant changes may be highlighted on our website. Your continued use of the Services after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your information? Reach us at liam@hedyverse.com.
